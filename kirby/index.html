Kirby: Nightmare in Dream Land

The peaceful world of Dream Land is in great danger.
In Dream Land, dreams had always flowed out of the wonderful Fountain of Dreams,
which the Fountain of Dreams collected the hopes and dreams of all living things.
It also provided all the good dreams and rest that come from deep sleep.
But one day, everyone in Dream Land lost the ability to dream!
Apparently, King DeDeDe had been bathing in the Fountain of Dreams!
Not only that, but DeDeDe had taken the Star Rod
— the source of power of the Fountain of Dreams —
and broken it into pieces, giving one to each of his underlings.
